About Us

Excellence in Education

Gurukulam Higher Secondary School is run with the principles of Brahmananda Swami Sivayogi, the Founder of Ananda Matham - the religion of bliss which advocates the importance of pure mind. Founded in 1972 by Swamy Nirmalananda Yogi, our mission is to instill great values along with quality education to provide better communal harmony.

Our Achievements:

  • 100% academic results since 2000
  • Best School Award - Kerala State Arts Festival (Kalolsavam) since 2010
  • Best School Award - Kerala State Work Experience 2018

Social Initiative

HOPE - Charity Program

Humans Offering Paradise On Earth (HOPE) is a student-run non-profit charity organization that has been making a difference for 10 years. Our students volunteer to support the needy in society, turning their compassion into action.

HOPE provides a platform for children to develop their social consciousness and contribute meaningfully to building a better world. This student wing functions under BSS Educational Society.
